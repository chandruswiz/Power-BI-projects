## Food Delivery Business Dashboard (Power BI)

### Objective
Analyze a food delivery business to understand revenue, orders, customer behavior, and performance trends.

### Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel (data cleaning)
- Basic business analysis

### Key Insights
- Total revenue and total orders overview
- Order trends by time and category
- Top-performing items and locations
- Customer demand patterns

### Business Value
This dashboard helps decision-makers:
- Identify high-revenue segments
- Improve operational efficiency
- Track business performance quickly
